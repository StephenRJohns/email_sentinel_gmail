## What the program does

emAIl Sentinel watches the account owner's **own** mailbox using plain-English rules. When an incoming email matches a rule, emAIl Sentinel sends a **one-way SMS alert** to a phone number the account owner entered themselves in its Settings.

- **Message category:** transactional account-notification alerts about email the recipient already receives — **not** marketing or promotional.
- **Who sends:** JJJJJ Enterprises, LLC, on behalf of the account owner who configured the rule. The sender and recipient are the same party — the account owner notifying themselves about their own email.
- **Who receives:** only phone numbers the account owner entered directly in the Settings (their own number, or a colleague's number they added with that colleague's permission). No numbers are sourced from third parties.
- **Direction:** one-way (alerts only). The program does not run conversations, marketing campaigns, or bulk outreach, and it never messages numbers a user did not enter.

emAIl Sentinel is offered as a free Gmail add-on, a free Microsoft 365 (Outlook) add-in, and a self-hosted application. The SMS behavior, opt-in, and disclosures described here are identical across all of them.

## How you opt in

Opt-in happens **inside emAIl Sentinel** — there is no web form and no keyword opt-in:

1. Install emAIl Sentinel (Gmail add-on, Outlook add-in, or self-hosted app).
2. Open **Settings**, choose your SMS provider, and manually enter your own mobile number as a recipient. The consent disclosure shown below appears directly beside the phone-number field.
3. Enable the **SMS** alert channel on a specific email rule.

Entering your number and enabling SMS on a rule constitutes your consent to receive recurring transactional alerts for email that matches that rule. **Consent is not a condition of purchase** — SMS is optional, and every other alert channel works without it.

## Program disclosures

- **Message frequency varies** — you receive a message only when an email matches one of your rules, so volume depends on your own rules and inbox.
- **Message and data rates may apply** — your mobile carrier's standard rates apply to messages you receive.
- **Opt out any time:** reply **STOP** to any message to stop all further alerts. You can also remove the number or disable the SMS channel in Settings.
- **Help:** reply **HELP** for help, or contact us at [support@jjjjjenterprises.com](mailto:support@jjjjjenterprises.com).
- **Consent is not a condition of purchase.** You are never required to enable SMS to install or use emAIl Sentinel.
- **Your opt-in data is never shared with third parties.** The mobile numbers you enter are not sold, rented, or shared with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes. Numbers are transmitted only to the SMS provider you configure, using your own credentials, solely to deliver the alerts you asked for. See Privacy Policy [§ 5](https://emailsentinel.jjjjjenterprises.com/legal/privacy.html).
